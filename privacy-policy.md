# Privacy Policy - AI Reply Notifier / 隐私政策 - AI 回复提醒助手

**Last updated / 最后更新：March 2026**

---

## English

### Overview
AI Reply Notifier is a browser extension that monitors AI chat pages and sends system notifications when a response is complete. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

### Data Collection
AI Reply Notifier does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing is done entirely on your local device.

### Permissions Used
- **notifications**: To send system alerts when AI responses complete
- **storage**: To save your preferences locally on your device only
- **tabs / scripting / activeTab**: To detect AI chat pages and monitor response status
- **alarms**: To periodically check response status in the background

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit user data.

### Third-Party Services
This extension does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

### Contact
If you have any questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

## 中文

### 概述
AI 回复提醒助手是一款浏览器扩展，用于在后台监控 AI 对话页面，并在回复完成时发送系统通知。我们承诺保护您的隐私。

### 数据收集
AI 回复提醒助手**不收集、不存储、不传输**任何个人数据。所有处理均在您的本地设备上完成。

### 权限说明
- **notifications（通知）**：用于在 AI 回复完成时发送系统提醒
- **storage（存储）**：仅用于在您的设备本地保存使用偏好设置
- **tabs / scripting / activeTab（标签页/脚本/活动标签页）**：用于检测 AI 对话页面并监控回复状态
- **alarms（定时器）**：用于在后台定期检查回复生成状态

以上权限均不用于收集或传输用户数据。

### 第三方服务
本扩展不接入任何第三方分析、广告或数据收集服务。

### 联系方式
如有任何疑问，请在我们的 GitHub 仓库中提交 Issue。
